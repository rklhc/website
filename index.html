    • 01PURPOSE

      “Actual Swaraj”

    • 02 OBJECT

      The main object of the “ Actual Swaraj ” is feel good by the people of India with regard to their physical, mental,socio-economic and legal health.Lot of measures have already been taken and further steps are being taken for strengthening the physical,mental and socio-economic health of people of this country ,but nothing tangible has been done towards the legal health corner except providing some financial assistance to economically backward people of India by way of legal aid as enshrined in the Constitution of India and the process of getting the said benefit is somewhat cumbersome.Voices are coming to bring gradual reforms to reduce the huge backlog of pending cases in Indian Courts subordinate as well as higher and number of increasing Judges with required proper infrastructure can be only a short-time measure which may cause heavy exchequer on Government. But the steps for reducing the filing of cases in Courts may be a permanent solution by way of settlement through specialist and legal health awareness compaign by creating National,State and Regional Legal Health Care Centre all over the country for the poor,downtroden and old aged people of India particularly the poor women,child and senior citizens of India who are the biggest sufferer in legal health care.

    • 03 REASONS FOR PROPOSAL

      Since I have been serving Bihar Government (Judicial Service) for the last 28 years , and I found that poor people of Bihar perticularly the women,child and senior citizens of weaker section of society are facing immense difficulties in settling their claims and rights in court due to procedural delay and which can be easily settled in legal health centres if it is run by the Government (centre as well as state)and Govt. of Bihar specifically, headed by Sri Nitish Kumar who has brought a splendid and remarkable changes in socio-economic and law & order situation and the world bank as well as the Congress Govt. in the centre have impressed with the turned around in Bihar's economy and law & order situation so why not the Nitish kumar's govt. should take a step in improving the legal health of people of Bihar and hope that Sri Nitish Kumar will take a step in creating legal health care centre in Patna as a state head authority and all the district of Bihar by bringing a Bihar Act 2010 to develop legal health services to eradicate the disease of filing cases and to reduce the mounting arrear of pending cases and to provide legal health care to poor and downtroden including BPL,APL by way of settlement out of courts as well as in courts through legal health awareness. The poor people of India are neither conscious of their rights nor they have resource for the remedy in case of their personal or property rights,particularly the victims of offences who remains waiting for timely justice for the reasons of their socio-economic backwardness and for want of proper legal health care as well as legal health awareness about their juris right.So the above people can be legally termed as “Juris Care Victims”.So, for the protection and welfare of health of juris care victims,an autonomous body has to be set-up as a legal health care hospital in every State Capital and all the Districts in phase manner to help the poor people of the country with special facility to the BPL and APL family and women,child,senior citizens,ex-serviceman,disabled and victims of terrorist activities to seek legal advice relating to the subject matter of disputes (pre-litigating and pending cases) and the legal health care hospital constituted for the purpose will help the party to settle the dispute in quick and amicable way and without any heavy economic burden under the Constitutional mandate “Right To Life”(under Article 21) and “Equal Justice” and “Free Legal Aid” (under Article 39-A of Indian Contitution and every State should take neccessary steps to implement the proposal by suitable legislation and the legal health should be made political issue and the resolution of disputes through amicable settlement by making people aware through legal health awareness compaign by creating National Rural Legal Health Mission to spread the object of the Legal Health from village to village. The Hon'ble President of India have recently expressed her serious concern and has given a call to make the justice cheeper and easily available for poor people of India on the naked fact of huge pending cases in different levels in the Courts and without legal aid it is not possible to go in the Courts for poor and downtroden people of India as the process of litigation has become very expensive and as such due to inequality in reaching the door step of justice ,two separate class can be created in the society for want of getting equal opportunity of justice and it will certainly obstruct the movement of development. The Hon'ble Prime Minister Dr. Manmohan Singh has also expressed his concern to deal with mounting arrears of court cases and bring down the litigation cost and said that Equality before the Law can not become a living reality if ordinary citizens do not have access to justice at affordable cost. The ex-chief justice Sri K.G.Blakrishnan has also laid emphasis on the mode of settlement by way of Conciliation and mediation etc. Hon'ble Chief Justice of India H.S.Kapadia has recently said that Fundamental Rights of citizens and Government's responsibilities could become meaningful onlywhen they were justiciable. The Hon'ble Law Minister Sri M Veerappa Moily recentely unveiled a National Litigation Policy with a view to ensure conduct of responsible litigation by the centre government and has urged every state govt. to evolve similar polocies and has said that the govt. would encourage arbitration and alternative dispute resolution means to ensure quicker justice out of courts. The root cause of legal health aliment of most of the people is due to lack of legal awareness and understanding about treatment by way of mutual settlement.The juris care victims can seek the advice through specialist of legal health care hospital to cure their legal health problem.
